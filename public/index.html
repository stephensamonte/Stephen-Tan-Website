About

I am a college student at Virginia Tech pursuing a degree in computer science.

I'm very passionate about technology and and building things that improve the lives of others. For example, when I was in high school I developed a mobile application that students can look to to keep up with events at the school. My senior year in high school I started learning machine learning and with my knowledge made created a self driving remote control car. Currently, here at Virginia Tech I live in the Innovate Living Learning Community and serve as a programmer to bring ideas to life.
